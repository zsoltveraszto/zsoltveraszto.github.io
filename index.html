Zsolt Veraszto

I am a fourth year PhD student in the Division of Applied Mathematics at Brown University, working in Govind Menon's group. I am broadly interested in dynamical systems and applications of differential geometry. More specifically, my current project is related to the generalizability of deep linear networks.
